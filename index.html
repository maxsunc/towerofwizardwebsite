Enter the Tower

Tower of Wizard is a survival rogue-lite where you ascend a cursed tower filled with monstrous foes and fallen wizards. Each run is a unique journey through 7 treacherous floors, where you'll collect powerful relics, craft broken builds, and gradually uncover the dark mystery that waits at the top.


Master over 40+ unique spells across 4 elemental schools. Face mini-bosses and floor guardians as you climb higher. Level up by collecting experience from defeated enemies in true vampire survivors fashion. Every floor brings new challenges, unique enemies, and valuable treasures to discover.

Frequently Asked Questions

Tower of Wizard will first launch on Kickstarter in Q4 2025. Following a successful campaign, we aim to release the game in Q2 2026.

Initially, Tower of Wizard will release on PC through Steam. Console ports (Nintendo Switch, PS5, Xbox Series X|S) are planned as stretch goals for our Kickstarter campaign.

The base game includes one main wizard character with multiple progression paths. Additional playable characters with unique abilities will be unlocked through gameplay achievements.

Tower of Wizard is primarily a single-player experience. Online co-op multiplayer is being considered as a post-launch feature.
