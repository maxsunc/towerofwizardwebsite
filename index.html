Tower of Wizard

Tower of Wizard is a survival rogue-lite where you ascend a cursed tower filled with monstrous foes and fallen wizards. Each run is a unique journey through 7 treacherous floors, where you'll collect powerful relics, craft broken builds, and gradually uncover the dark mystery that waits at the top.


Master over 40+ unique spells across 4 elemental schools. Face mini-bosses and floor guardians as you climb higher. Level up by collecting experience from defeated enemies in true vampire survivors fashion. Every floor brings new challenges, unique enemies, and valuable treasures to discover.

Hi! I'm Max Sun ☀️, I've been developing this game part-time for the past six months while studying CE at university. Even with a full class schedule, I dedicate 5–7 hours a day to bring this vision to life. I'm also collaborating with my talented classmate Lucas Robinson, who handles the art side of things!

Rogue-lite and wizard games are my genre of video games, so I want to make the coolest wizard rogue-lite there is! This game is also the sequal to the first-ever game I published on Itch 5 years ago, so in many ways it's a love letter to game development :).
